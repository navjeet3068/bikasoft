Our Story

From Code to Culture.

Bikasoft was founded on the principle that technology should be a force multiplier for business. We saw a gap between traditional agencies that delivered static websites and the complex needs of modern, growing companies. Our mission is to bridge that gap by providing end-to-end digital systems—from brand strategy and software engineering to AI automation and growth marketing—that create measurable, long-term value.
